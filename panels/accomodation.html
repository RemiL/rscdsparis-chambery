Travel to Vichy

There is a small international airport, Clermont-Auvergne, at Aulnat but with limited flight destinations. Most travellers by plane will fly to either Paris or Lyon and take a train.

Six direct train services a day link Vichy to Paris (2h50) and Lyon (2h15). See train company websites: TGV Europe or from the UK SNCF, the French national train company.

Driving time from Paris to Vichy is around 3h30m and from Lyon 2h00.

Accommodation

Vichy, being a popular spa town, offers numerous hotels both in the town itself and in the surrounding countryside.

You will find below those hotels which are within walking distance of the Palais des Congrès. As an indication of price, current rates per room are shown for two people sharing. If you are travelling by car, few hotels have their own car park but public car parking is available at around 7€/10€ per night or check with the hotel on the ease of street parking.

Note that Easter marks the beginning of the tourist season, so it is strongly recommended that you reserve hotel accommodation well in advance.
