Easter week-end in Vichy
for advanced dancers

Every four years, the RSCDS Paris Branch organises an Easter dance week-end for 3 days for advanced dancers. Join us in 2016, from the 26th to 28th March, in the beautiful spa town of Vichy, in Auvergne in the centre of France, and dance in the Palais des Congrès, with its impressive architecture, build by Napoleon III in the 19th century, and situated in six hectares of park in the centre of the town.
