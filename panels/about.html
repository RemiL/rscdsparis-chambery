Easter weekend in Vichy
for advanced dancers

Every four years, the RSCDS Paris Branch organises an international Easter dance weekend for advanced dancers. Join us in 2016, from the 26th to 28th March, in the beautiful spa town of Vichy, in Auvergne in the centre of France. Come and dance in the impressive Palais des Congrès, build by Napoleon III in the 19th century.

Teacher

Graham Donald

Musicians

Muriel Johnstone (piano), Ian Robertson (fiddle), Jim Lindsay (accordion)

Venue

Palais des Congrès, 5 Rue du Casino, Vichy

Classes, dances and meals will be in the Palais des Congrès, except for Sunday lunch which will be in the restaurant chez Aletti situated opposite the Palais des Congrès.
