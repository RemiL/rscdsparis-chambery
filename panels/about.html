Easter weekend in Chambéry
for advanced dancers

Every four years, the RSCDS Paris Branch organises an international Easter dance weekend for 3 days for advanced dancers. Join us in 2020, from the 11th to 13th April, in Chambéry, in the French Alps, the historic capital of the Savoy.

Teacher

Ron WALLACE

Musicians

Sarah-Jane SUMMERS (fiddle)
James GRAY (piano)

Venue

Centre des Congrès Le Manège, 331 rue de la République, Chambéry

Classes, dances and meals will be in the Centre des Congrès, except for the gala dinner on Sunday evening which will be in the Domaine des Saints Pères, a 15‑minute coach trip from the Centre des Congrès.

A number of hotels are available within walking distance of the venue. A special deal has been arranged with the Hotel Kyriad, situated next to the Centre des Congrès.

Public parking is available next to the Centre des Congrès at a cost of 9.50€ per day. Day tickets can purchased from the Hotel Kyriad or from the Centre de Congrès.
