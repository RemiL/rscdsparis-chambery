Travelling to Chambéry

Travellers by plane can fly to either Paris, Lyon or Geneva and take a train or shuttle bus to Chambéry.

Fairly frequent train services link Chambéry to Paris (3h), Lyon (1h) and Geneva (2h) and trains can also be taken directly from these airports.
See train company website: Oui.SNCF.
