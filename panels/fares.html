Cancellation policy

Cancellation policy for the Weekend excluding Options:

    • Cancellation and replacement from waiting list: Full refund less 30€ administration charge.
    • Cancellation up to 28 March 2020 and no replacement from waiting list: Refund 135€.
    • Cancellation after 28 March 2020 and no replacement from waiting list: No refund

Cancellation policy for Options:

    • Cancellation of Gala Dinner up to 28 March 2020: Full refund less 5€ administration charge.
    • Cancellation of Friday Extra up to 28 March 2020: Full refund less 5€ administration charge.
    • Cancellation after 28 March 2020: No refund

Participants are very strongly advised to make their own arrangements to insure against cancellation through illness, previous injury or other unavoidable circumstances.
